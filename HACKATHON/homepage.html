About ST Peter's Hospital

ST Peter's Hospital is one of the best private hospitals in Nigeria. We have been in existence since 2015. At ST Peter's Hospital, we have highly qualified health professionals including general practionioners, specialist and consultant doctors, nurses and midwives, professional laboratory scientists, radiographers and technicians and occupational health officers. Our departments includes:

  • Hematology Medical laboratory
  • Histopathology Laboratory
  • Immunohematology Laboratory (Blood bank)
  • Radiological and Diagnostic Department
  • Dentistry Department
  • Obstetrics and Gynecology Department
  • Oncology Department
  • Urology Department
  • Therapy
  • Physiotherapy
    • Our motto is to deliver quality healthcare services as we put our patients first.
